SubmissionsMiddleware

public final class SubmissionsMiddleware : Middleware

Middleware that intercepts and SubmissionValidationErrors and renders them as Responses.

Note: needs to come after ErrorMiddleware (if present) to avoid the SubmissionValidationErrors from being transformed into Internal Server errors on the way back up the responder chain.
